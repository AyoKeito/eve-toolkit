Epic arcs

Handcrafted mission chains spanning entire regions of New Eden. Each arc is repeatable every 3 months and awards large faction standing payouts — the standard route to repairing or building empire standings. Pirate arcs also drop exclusive ship blueprint copies.

COSMOS Planned

COSMOS missions are constellation-bound, one-time mission chains tied to landmarks scattered across each empire's space. Unlike epic arcs they cannot be repeated — each agent grants their rewards only once per character, making them a strategic permanent source of faction and corp standings.

Coverage is planned for all four empire constellations, listing agents by constellation, required skills, reward items, and the permanent standing impact.
